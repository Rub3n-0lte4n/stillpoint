How Stillpoint works

When you read normally, your eyes don't glide along the line — they jump, stop, and often dart back to re-read. Stillpoint removes the chase: it holds each word at one fixed point, aligned on the letter your eye naturally locks onto. You stay still; the words come to you.

One word at a timeEach word centred on its focal letter — the calmest, most precise way to read. ORP
In small phrasesA few words at once, for a faster, more natural rhythm. RSVP
A blend of bothShort phrases with the focal anchor kept in place. Hybrid

Everything runs inside your browser. Your files are parsed and read on this device and never uploaded — no account, no server, nothing tracked.
